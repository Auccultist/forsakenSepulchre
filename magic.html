MAGIC

Magic is a major component of The Forsaken Sepulchre and it's characters. Below, you can find pages on the different types of magic and the abilities that can be given to your character.

Each magic has it's own abilities and it's own culture. Your character may only practice one magic. You cannot have different abilities from different classes.

*Note about ability points: (â˜…) is the number of ability points something is worth. Your character starts off with 3 ability points that you can 'spend' to give them different abilities. You can earn more through levelling up your character.
If your character is a lvl 2 in an ability, they can also use the lvl 1 version of their ability.
NPCs(story characters) are not limited by ability points.
